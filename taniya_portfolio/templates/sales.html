📈 Sales Dashboard

Project Overview

This Power BI Sales Dashboard analyzes product sales, selling price, product cost, expected profit and state-wise performance using interactive charts and KPIs.

Dashboard Features

  • Sum of Selling Price by Product
  • Count of Product ID by Product State
  • Count of Product by Selling Price
  • Expected Profit by Order Date
