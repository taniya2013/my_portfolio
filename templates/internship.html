💡 My Motivation

As an Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning student, I have always believed that learning extends beyond the classroom. While academic studies provided me with a strong theoretical foundation, I wanted to gain practical experience by working on real-world projects and understanding how technology is applied in the industry.

This motivation encouraged me to join the 6 Weeks AI & ML Internship at CodroidHub Pvt. Ltd., where I had the opportunity to enhance my technical skills, explore modern development tools, and work on practical applications in a collaborative learning environment.

I am also sincerely grateful to JMIETI for encouraging students to participate in industry-oriented internships. The continuous support and guidance from my college helped me connect academic knowledge with practical experience and strengthened my confidence as an aspiring AI & ML professional.

🏢 About the Internship

During my 6-week internship at CodroidHub Pvt. Ltd., I gained practical exposure to Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Python Development, Web Scraping, Flask, APIs, and Data Analysis. The internship emphasized hands-on learning, allowing me to transform theoretical concepts into real-world applications through various projects and assignments.

📅 Weekly Learning Journey

Week 1 — Foundation & Introduction

Started the internship by understanding the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Python programming, and the internship workflow. I also became familiar with GitHub and the overall development environment.

Week 2 — Web Development with Flask

Learned Flask framework, routing, templates, HTML, CSS, and created dynamic web applications while understanding how frontend and backend communicate.

Week 3 — Web Scraping

Worked on multiple web scraping projects using BeautifulSoup and Selenium. Extracted data from different websites and displayed it in Flask applications.

Week 4 — APIs & Data Handling

Integrated REST APIs, handled JSON responses, processed data using Python, and built interactive API-based applications.

Week 5 — Machine Learning & Data Analysis

Explored Machine Learning concepts, data preprocessing, visualization, model building, and practical implementation using Python libraries.

Week 6 — Capstone Project & Portfolio

Successfully completed the capstone project, improved my portfolio, integrated multiple projects, and presented the final work showcasing the skills gained throughout the internship.
